Settings Introduction

In tool 'Settings', you can:

  • Choose different background maps
  • Choose different weapon styles
  • Change the size and opacity of the target
  • Change the color of the target
Modes Introduction

To use tool 'GameMode', you ought to know:

  • What kind of practice do you need
  • Your current skill level
  • Your target skill level

After that:

  • Choose the mode that suits you
  • Adjust the difficulty level that suits you

Modes Detail:

  • warming-up:
    In this mode, the target will disappear after 1.5 seconds, which is a simple warm-up mode.
  • fast-click:
    In this mode, the target will gradually become transparent and difficult to see before disappearing. Adjust the difficulty to train your reflexes and positioning skills!
  • exact-remember:
    In this mode, the target will become invisible after a period of time, remember its position and shoot it, adjust the difficulty to exercise your memory ability!
  • In every mode,the train lasts 60s.
